OUR STORY

Where Tradition
Meets Perfection

Matsuki was founded with a single vision—to bring the soul of authentic Japanese cuisine to Addis Ababa. Every dish reflects generations of culinary artistry, crafted with carefully selected ingredients and unwavering attention to detail.

Inspired by the philosophy of Omotenashi, our hospitality goes beyond service. Every guest is welcomed with warmth, every ingredient is treated with respect, and every meal becomes an unforgettable experience.
